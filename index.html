Lumenaria is implementing ten test phases before mainnet launch. Current test phase is 2/10.


If you want to join us in testing lumenARIA, please send an email to lumenARIA.join.test@proton.me with the subject “Request to join lumenARIA test”. The provided email address will be used to contact you when needed.


In the body of the email, please provide a never-used self-custodied Externally Owned Address for which you have the private key.


lumenARIA will use this address to send you a nominal amount of Sepolia (testnet) ETH on L1 to facilitate your testing of the lumenARIA software.


To participate in the test program, lumenARIA requires your full legal name, country, we may also contact you for additional verification purposes and request more information as needed to ensure the integrity of the lumenARIA test program.


For the initial test phases, lumenARIA will contact you with your provided email address and provide download links and instructions as we are ready for you to participate in the testing.

lumenARIA reserves the right to reject or terminate participants from the test program if we deem those participants are abusing the trust of the project, failing to participate in lumenARIA testing as requested, present an unacceptable legal risk to lumenARIA (eg. North Korean nationality) or are otherwise disruptive to the project.


lumenARIA is a fair launch Ethereum infrastructure project owned entirely by the Ethereum community and by the lumenARIA participants in test and production phases. lumenARIA test program ownership distribution will be on a pro-rata basis for participants in each phase.
