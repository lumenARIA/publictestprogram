Ethereum Public Goods Infrastructure (EPGi) Test Program

Current test phase: 5 / 10

Ethereum Public Goods Infrastructure (EPGi) is implementing ten test phases before mainnet launch. Current test phase is 5/10.

If you want to join us in testing EPGi, please send an email to lumenARIA.join.test@proton.me with the subject “Request to join lumenARIA test”. The provided email address will be used to contact you when needed.

In the body of the email, please provide a never-used self-custodied Externally Owned Address for which you have the private key.

EPGi will use this address to send you a nominal amount of Sepolia (testnet) ETH on L1 to facilitate your testing of the EPGi software.

To participate in the test program, EPGi requires your full legal name, country, we may also contact you for additional verification purposes and request more information as needed to ensure the integrity of the lumenARIA test program.

For the initial test phases, EPGi will contact you with your provided email address and provide download links and instructions as we are ready for you to participate in the testing.

Please include the following in your email:

New Ethereum Address (EOA) for EPGi to use for you to send Sepolia testnet ETH and to airdrop your ARIA tokens on mainnet launch (TGE)

Full legal name

Country

Your available platforms for use with testing EPGi (select all that apply):

  • Windows
  • Macintosh
  • Linux
  • Other Unix (please specify)
  • iOS
  • Android

What is your interest in EPGi and the goals of this project?

(Please tell us in your email)

EPGi reserves the right to reject or terminate participants from the test program if we deem those participants are abusing the trust of the project, failing to participate in lumenARIA testing as requested, present an unacceptable legal risk to EPGi (eg. North Korean nationality) or are otherwise disruptive to the project.

EPGi will never disclose any information provided to us, and we will destroy all personally identifying information provided by test program participants upon mainnet launch.

EPGi is a fair launch Ethereum infrastructure project owned entirely by the Ethereum community and by the EPGi participants in test and production phases. EPGi test program ownership distribution will be on a pro-rata basis for participants in each phase.

Thank you for your interest in helping test EPGi!
